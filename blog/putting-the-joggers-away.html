putting the joggers away

14th march 2022

in my last post i talked about my main new year's resolution for work of doing less. but i also have another, even simpler one. and it's already done me a world of good and is much easier to measure. but first, a little bit of background...

since near the start of the pandemic in the uk, i've been working from home. and with that has come a huge change in my habits. one of them for example, is that i no longer needed to get properly dressed in the morning. as i wasn't heading into a place with other humans that i worked with, i have been able to slip further and further away from what most would consider, clothes suitable for the outside world.

now i never went as far as working in my pyjamas or underwear. but i definitely started to put less and less effort into what i wore whilst working from home. i opted for comfier clothes, optimising for function over fashion.

but in this, i think lost something. i can't quite put my finger on what. but there's a mental switch that gets flicked as soon as i put jeans or trousers on. there's an attitude change that occurs when i shave and do my hair. it made me feel more awake, attentive, engaged and generally less slobbish. it also helped with the separation between work and home.

so with that i decided to put the joggers away this year.
