Hello, this is my README page. I was inspired by Jordan's README.

How I like to work

  • I aim to "Lead in the open" as much as possible
  • Strong ownership boundaries ensure people feel empowered to make changes within their area
  • Teams should be open to contributions of any form. Be that code, feedback, documentation
  • I prefer to reduce process down to the core values. I like the classic "people over process" principle
  • I enjoy chaos. And I am being encouraged to embrace this chaos. Teams and ICs work well when they are free to move at their own pace and in their own direction.
  • I feel job titles and roles can be flexible. I like the engineers I work with to be able to influence product, project management, UX etc.
